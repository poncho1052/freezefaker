# Freeze Faker Trailer Script v0.1

This is an early trailer structure for store and social use.

---

## 1. Core Trailer Goal

The trailer must communicate the game in under 10 seconds.

Core viewer takeaway:

> A crowd freezes. Some of them are human players. You have to spot who looks too human.

---

## 2. 30-Second Steam Trailer Structure

### 0:00〜0:03 — Establish Crowd
Visual:

- Bright station-front shopping street
- NPCs walking naturally
- Fakers blended in

Text:

```text
A city full of NPCs.
```

---

### 0:03〜0:06 — Introduce Rule
Visual:

- Signal changes
- Red Light warning
- Crowd freezes at once

Text:

```text
Red Light: Freeze.
```

---

### 0:06〜0:10 — Twist
Visual:

- Several frozen characters
- One suspicious pose
- Watcher camera zooms in

Text:

```text
But don’t just stop.
Stop like an NPC.
```

---

### 0:10〜0:15 — Watcher Gameplay
Visual:

- Watcher scans crowd
- Pins suspicious character
- Accuses

Text:

```text
Spot the human.
```

---

### 0:15〜0:20 — Comedy / False Accusation
Visual:

- Watcher accuses wrong NPC
- Penalty appears
- NPC remains blank-faced

Text:

```text
Accuse wisely.
```

---

### 0:20〜0:25 — Faker Survival
Visual:

- Faker survives right in front of Watcher
- Faker reaches goal

Text:

```text
Blend in. Survive. Escape.
```

---

### 0:25〜0:30 — Logo End Card
Visual:

- Freeze Faker logo
- Red / Green signal accents
- Crowd freeze background

Text:

```text
Freeze Faker
Don’t Act Human.
```

---

## 3. 15-Second Short Video Structure

### 0:00〜0:02
Crowd walking.

Text:

```text
この中に人間がいます
```

### 0:02〜0:04
Red Light triggers. Everyone freezes.

Text:

```text
全員停止
```

### 0:04〜0:08
Camera slowly pans across suspicious poses.

Text:

```text
誰が人間かわかる？
```

### 0:08〜0:12
Watcher accuses.

Text:

```text
正解？それとも誤指摘？
```

### 0:12〜0:15
Reveal / logo.

Text:

```text
Freeze Faker
Don’t Act Human.
```

---

## 4. Trailer Must-Show Moments

- Crowd moving naturally
- Red Light signal
- Full crowd freeze
- Suspicious posture
- Watcher zoom / accuse
- False accusation
- Faker reaching goal
- End-of-round identity reveal

---

## 5. Trailer Tone

Should feel:

- Fast to understand
- Funny
- Tense
- Streamable
- Social deduction-like
- Lightly surreal

Should not feel:

- Horror
- Tactical shooter
- Overly serious
- Generic prop hunt

---

## 6. Voiceover Draft

English:

```text
In Freeze Faker, anyone in the crowd could be human.
When the light turns red, everyone freezes.
But stopping is not enough.
You have to stop like an NPC.
Blend in, watch closely, and don’t act human.
```
