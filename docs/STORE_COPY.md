Blend into an NPC crowd. Freeze when the Red Light hits. But don’t just stop — stop like an NPC. Freeze Faker is a party game of observation, deception, and suspiciously human body language.

## 5. Longer Japanese Description

Freeze Fakerは、NPCの群衆に紛れた人間プレイヤーを見抜く、観察型かくれんぼパーティーゲームです。

Fakerは、駅前商店街やショッピングモールのような日常の群衆に紛れ、ゴールやミッション達成を目指します。Green Light中は少しずつ移動できますが、Red Lightが鳴った瞬間、街の全員が一斉に停止します。

ただし、止まれば安全とは限りません。

Watcherは、止まり方、目線、距離感、姿勢、立ち位置の微妙な違和感を観察し、群衆の中に潜む人間を指摘します。

NPCっぽく歩き、NPCっぽく止まり、NPCっぽく存在できるか。

あなたは人間っぽさを消せますか？

---

## 6. Longer English Description

Freeze Faker is an observation-based hide-and-seek party game where human players hide among crowds of NPCs.

As a Faker, you move through everyday urban crowds, complete objectives, and try to reach the goal. During Green Light, you can move. But when Red Light hits, everyone freezes.

Stopping is not enough.

The Watcher studies the crowd and looks for suspiciously human details: posture, gaze, spacing, body angle, and the tiny mistakes people make when trying to act natural.

Walk like an NPC. Freeze like an NPC. Don’t Act Human.

## 10. Notes

Do not market the game as a horror title.

Do not over-explain the mechanics before showing the freeze moment.

The strongest hook is visual:

> A whole crowd freezes, and the viewer wants to guess who is human.
